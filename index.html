Privacy Policy

UPSKILL collects only the data needed to provide personalised career guidance. All data is stored encrypted using AES-256 at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit.

You may export or delete your data at any time (right to erasure). Resume files are retained for a maximum of 12 months unless deleted earlier.

We never sell your personal data to third parties. Anonymised, aggregated data may be used to improve our AI models.

UPSKILL is fully compliant with India's DPDP Act 2023 and is GDPR-ready. Explicit consent is obtained before any data processing.
